Inline Water Quality Monitor

  • Category: Sensor Based Monitoring
  • Client: EKKI Pumps
  • Project date: 01 March, 2022

Product detail

  • Eight water quality indicators, including pH, temperature, flow, turbidity, TSS, TDS, BOD, and COD, are measured using four sensors with built-in artificial intelligence.
  • All measurement data available in a click thanks to digital interfaces and an easily read display.
  • Compact and modular construction.
